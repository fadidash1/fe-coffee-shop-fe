Our Story

Crafting Coffee Since 2010

Founded by two coffee enthusiasts with a passion for quality, Brew Haven started as a small cart in the farmers market. Today, we're proud to serve our community with carefully sourced beans and artisanal brewing techniques.

Direct Trade Coffee

We work directly with farmers to ensure fair prices and sustainable practices.

Small Batch Roasting

Our beans are roasted in small batches for optimal flavor and freshness.

Community Focused

We host weekly events and support local artists and musicians.
